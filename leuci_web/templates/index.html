Leucippus - Electron Density Map Browser

An application to explore electron density in x-ray crystal structures and cryo-EM structures
This application faciliates the exploration of the electron density of x-ray and cryo-EM structures, exploring the 3d matrices of their electron distributions.
The application displays contour plots through the matrix to see the distributions of electrons in chosen positions. You can select either xyz coorinates in space or residues/atoms to explore.

Leucippus, the Greek Philosopher

Leucippus, a pre-Socratic Greek philosopher, is considered to be the first philosopher to develop the theory of atomism. Atomism is a natural philosphpy that proposes that the physical universe is made up of indivisible components called atoms. Between atoms there is void, which is the possible space that atoms can (or do) occupy. Leucippus' student, Democritus, gained far more fame both then and now. Leucippus' work is known through the writings of Democritus. Information taken from Wikipedia Stanford
